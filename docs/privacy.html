Privacy Policy

SubWake - Subscription Renewal Reminder Manager

Last updated: May 6, 2025

Zero Data Collection Promise: SubWake does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All your subscription data stays on your device.

1. Data We Collect

SubWake collects no personal data. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial information (credit card numbers, bank details)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data

2. Data Storage

All subscription data you enter is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account via CloudKit, which Apple manages under its own privacy policy.

3. Third-Party Services

SubWake does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app functions entirely offline with no server communication.

4. Notifications

SubWake uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming subscription renewals. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any server communication.

5. Widgets

Home screen widgets display your subscription data directly from local storage. No data is transmitted externally to populate widgets.

6. Children's Privacy

SubWake does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, our app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual subscriptions within the app
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes all local data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
