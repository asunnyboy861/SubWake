SubWake - Subscription Renewal Reminder Manager

Getting Started

Add Your First Subscription

Tap the "+" tab to add a new subscription. Enter the service name, price, billing cycle, and renewal date. SubWake will automatically calculate your monthly and yearly spending.

Enable Notifications

Go to Settings and enable "Reminders" to receive timely notifications before your subscriptions renew. You can customize how many days in advance to be reminded.

Track Free Trials

When adding a subscription, toggle "Is Free Trial" and set the trial end date. SubWake will remind you before the trial converts to a paid subscription.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does SubWake access my bank or payment accounts?

No. SubWake does not connect to any financial services. You manually enter subscription details, and all data stays on your device.

How does iCloud Sync work?

When enabled in Settings, your subscription data syncs across all your Apple devices using your iCloud account. Data is encrypted end-to-end by Apple.

Can I customize reminder timing?

Yes! For each subscription, you can choose to be reminded 1, 3, 7, 14, or 30 days before the renewal date.

What happens if I miss a cancellation deadline?

SubWake sends multiple reminders before cancellation deadlines. However, you are responsible for actually canceling subscriptions with the service provider.
