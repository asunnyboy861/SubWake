# Pricing Configuration

## Monetization Model: Paid Download

- **Price**: $3.99 (One-time purchase)
- **IAP**: Not required
- **Subscription**: None

## App Store Connect Pricing
- **Price Tier**: Tier 3 ($3.99)

## Competitive Pricing Analysis
| App | Price | Model |
|-----|-------|-------|
| Rocket Money | $6-12/month | Subscription |
| Bobby | $2.99 | One-time |
| SubscriptMe | Subscription | Subscription |
| Subby | $9.99 | One-time |
| SubSorted | Free+IAP | Freemium |
| **SubWake** | **$3.99** | **One-time** |

## Why One-Time Purchase
- Avoids the "subscription irony" (subscription tracker that is itself a subscription)
- Users hate paying monthly for a tracker
- $3.99 is competitive: more than Bobby ($2.99) but less than Subby ($9.99)
- No ongoing server costs (all data local, iCloud sync is free)
- Higher trust: privacy-first app should not have recurring billing

## Policy Pages Required
- Support Page: Yes
- Privacy Policy: Yes
- Terms of Use: No (Not needed for paid download apps)
