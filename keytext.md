The average person pays for 10+ subscriptions every month. Free trials expire and auto-convert. Prices creep up without notice. Cancellation deadlines slip by. You end up paying for services you barely use.

THE SOLUTION
SubWake gives you complete visibility and control over all your subscriptions with smart reminders, spending insights, and cancellation tracking.

KEY FEATURES

Smart Tiered Reminders
Get notified before renewals with customizable alerts at 3, 7, 14, or 30 days. Never be caught off guard by an unexpected charge again.

Free Trial Alerts
Set separate reminders for trial expirations at 1, 3, or 7 days before they convert to paid. Decide on your terms, not theirs.

Cancellation Deadline Tracking
Track notice periods and cancellation windows. SubWake counts down the days so you never miss a deadline.

Price Change Monitoring
Get notified when a subscription price increases. Spot hidden hikes and make informed decisions about whether to keep or cancel.

Spending Dashboard
See your total monthly and yearly subscription costs at a glance. Understand exactly where your money goes.

Home Screen Widget
Check upcoming renewals and spending totals right from your home screen with customizable widgets.

iCloud Sync
Optionally sync your data across all your Apple devices. Your subscriptions, your choice.

Privacy First
SubWake collects zero data. No bank connections. No account required. Everything stays on your device unless you choose iCloud sync.
